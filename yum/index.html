LEMON CHEESECAKE
Best served as a desert at a sunday barbeque!

CRUST

1 3/4 C vanilla wafer crumbs 1/4 C unsalted melted butter

FILLING

  • 3 (8 oz.) packages cream cheese
  • 1 C fat free sour cream
  • 2 C sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 t lemon zest
  • 2 T lemon juice

    LEMON CURD

  • 1 ½ t lemon zest
  • 6 T lemon juice
  • 1 large egg and yolk
  • ½ C sugar
  • 2 T butter cut into bits
    • 1. To make the crust preheat oven to 325˚F. Spray a 9 in. pan with cooking spray. Mix crumbs and melted butter in a bowl. Press crumb mixture onto bottom and 1 ½ in. up on the side of pan. Bake for 8 – 10 mins. or until edges are lightly golden and crust is set. Cool on rack.
    • 2. To make the filling beat cream cheese and sour cream until fluffy with an electric mixer on medium high. Gradually beat in sugar then eggs, 1 at a time. Beat in zest and juice. Pour into crust, bake for 1 hour and 15 mins. or until center is almost set. Let cool completely.
    • 3. To make the lemon curd combine lemon zest, lemon juice, egg, egg yolk, and suga in the top of a double boiler over simmering water. Whisk until hot and frothy. Gradually whisk in butter until thickened. Let cool for 30 mins.
    • 4. Trasnfer to a serving plate and spread lemon curd over the top. Let stand at room temp. for 30 mins. then cool in refrigirator overnight before serving.
