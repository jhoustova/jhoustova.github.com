CRUST

  • 1/2 C unsalted butter
  • 1/4 C confectioner's sugar
  • 1 C all purpose flour
  • 1/8 t salt


FILLING

  • 1/3 C fresh lemon juice
  • 1 T grated lemon zest
  • 1 C of white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 T flour

CRUST

  1. 1. Preheat oven to 350˚F. Grease 8 × 8 in. pan with non stick vegetable spray. 2. With an electric mixer or hand mixer, beat butter and powdered sugar until creamy and smooth. Add flour and salt and beat until the dough starts to come together. 3. Press dough onto the bottom of pan and bake for 18 – 20 mins. or until lightly brown around the edges. Let cool while you make the filling.

FILLING

  1. 1. With an electric mixer or hand mixer, mix granulated sugar and eggs until nice and smooth. 2. Add the lemon juice and lemon zest and stir to combine. Fold in flour. Then pour the filling over shortbread crust and bake for 17 – 20 mins. or until filling is set then let it cool. 3. To serve cut into 2 in. squares and dust with powdered sugar.
