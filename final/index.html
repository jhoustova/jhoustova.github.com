ABOUT ME

My name is Milly Houstova and I am a student at Massachusetts College of Art and Design. I have gained skills in typography, print and interactive design. I can intergrate concept, innovation and creativity into any design project. I can also construct easy, user friendly interactive applications but I enjoy creating images through typography the most.
